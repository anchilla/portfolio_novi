Silmarilion Kristali Website

Silmarilion Kristali

Technologies: HTML, CSS, JavaScript. PHP was used to connect to a server.
Description:
This website is my first freelance project. It's made to be responsive and can be viewed by a variety of devices, such as mobile phones, tablets, laptops, PCs and more. It's made for a company Silmarilion Kristali, a business based in Belgrade, Serbia. They sell jewelry made of gemstones and provide hand made products. The website showcases their products and services and information about how they started. There's a search option as well, which helps the customer to find an item if they are looking for something in particular. Every page is designed to be aesthetically pleasing and easy to navigate through.
